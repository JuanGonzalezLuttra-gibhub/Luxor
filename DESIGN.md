# Design System: Luxor Auto

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#000000` (Matte Black) - Core background and high-contrast elements.
- **Secondary**: `#121212` (Anthracite) - Card backgrounds and subtle sections.
- **Accent**: `#C0C0C0` (Metallic Silver) - Call to actions, borders, and premium highlights.
- **Support**: `#E5E4E2` (Platinum) - Secondary highlights and hover states.
- **Neutral**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Typography and essential iconography.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Inter` or `Outfit` - Bold, uppercase, spacious.
- **Body**: `Inter` - Clean, readable, focused on clarity.

## Visual Language
- **Imagery**: Cinematic, high-contrast, moody lighting, shallow depth of field.
- **Components**: Large border radii (16px+), subtle glassmorphism (`backdrop-filter`), thin gold borders.
- **Animations**: Fade-ins on scroll, gentle scaling hover effects on car cards.
